# ShopSphere FAQ

## Account & orders

**How do I create an account?**
Go to shopsphere.com/signup and enter your email + password. Account creation is free and gives you faster checkout, order history, and saved addresses.

**How do I reset my password?**
Visit shopsphere.com/account/reset and enter the email associated with your account. A reset link is valid for 30 minutes.

**How do I enable two-factor authentication?**
After signing in, go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. We support both authenticator apps (TOTP) and SMS.

**Where can I see my order history?**
Sign in and go to "My Orders". Orders older than 24 months are archived but still accessible by entering the order number.

**Can I cancel an order after placing it?**
Yes, if it has not yet shipped. Go to "My Orders" → select the order → Cancel. After shipment, you must use the returns process instead.

## Payments

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ShopSphere Gift Cards. Cryptocurrency is **not** accepted.

**Why was my card declined?**
Common reasons: insufficient funds, address mismatch, expired card, or fraud-prevention hold by your bank. Try a different card or contact your bank.

**Do you offer financing?**
Yes — Affirm financing is available for orders over $100, with terms of 3, 6, or 12 months.

## Customer support

**How do I contact support?**
- Email: support@shopsphere.com (response within 24 hours on weekdays)
- Phone: 1-800-SHOPSPH, Mon-Fri 9am-7pm ET
- Live chat: available on shopsphere.com Mon-Fri 9am-9pm ET, weekends 10am-6pm ET

**Is there a loyalty program?**
Yes — ShopSphere Plus. $9.99/month for free express shipping on every order, 5% back as store credit, and early access to sales.

## Privacy & data

**What data does ShopSphere collect?**
We collect order details, account info, browsing/usage analytics, and payment metadata (we do **not** store full card numbers). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

**Can I delete my account?**
Yes — email privacy@shopsphere.com from the email on file. Account and personal data are deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by tax or accounting law.
